## Overview

This module covers the NVIDIA Isaac ecosystem for developing intelligent robotic systems, focusing on simulation, perception, and navigation capabilities.

## Module Structure

The AI-Robot Brain module consists of three chapters:

1. **Chapter 1: NVIDIA Isaac Sim** - Understanding photorealistic simulation for robot training
2. **Chapter 2: Isaac ROS (VSLAM & Perception)** - Implementing visual SLAM and perception systems
3. **Chapter 3: Nav2 Navigation Stack** - Configuring autonomous navigation for humanoid robots
